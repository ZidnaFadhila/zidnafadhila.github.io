Writing Skills

Experienced in doing writing, both fictional and non-fictional. Have some experience writing short movies and theatrical script. Writing is a side job and a hobby to spend spare time. I love writing!

Fictional
Short Story : Tarian Rindu (2018)

A short story about a little girl with mental disability that attempts to achieve her dream of becoming traditional dancer. This story is published in a short story anthology book by SMA Negeri 2 Bantul. 

Theatrical Script
Impian Diatas Kanvas Putih (2019)

Script that plays in theatrical performances. Tells about Tathaka, a young man who aspired to be a painter. Struggling to achieved his dream, despite opposition from those around him. This theatrical was performed on Expert and SMADABA CUP 2019 SMA Negeri 2 Bantul. 

Short Movie Script

Jatimurti (2019)

Jatimurti (2019)

Have experience as a co-writer for short movie scripts, collaboration with Amananta Arabian. The film was shown in a short film competition, the Bantul Film Festival 2019 held by Dinas Kebudayaan Bantul. This short local movie tells about a mythical romantic encounter between founder of the Mataram Sultanate and the Queen of the South Coast. The story became a legend embodied in ritual form in the Cepuri area, Parangkusumo, Bantul.
